Grid Mood is a Week 4 multi-service system: a Next.js + Tailwind frontend on Vercel, a Node.js Railway worker polling WattTime every 30 to 60 seconds, and Supabase for Postgres, Auth, and Realtime. The worker writes current grid-state data with upsert; the frontend subscribes via Realtime and renders a live atmospheric artwork driven by WattTime signals per chosen place continuously.
